About Me

I’m a hybrid developer–creator working at the intersection of Generative AI, agentic systems, multimedia storytelling, and creative technology. I design and build systems that combine LLMs, RAG pipelines, data analytics, conversational agents, voice AI, image generation, and interactive media.

My work explores how humans and AI can co‑create — from business intelligence dashboards and agentic assistants to comics, music, and voice‑driven experiences.

  • Focus: Generative AI · RAG · Agents · Creative Workflows

  • Background: Early virtual world AI, defense research, AI education, and creative tech.
